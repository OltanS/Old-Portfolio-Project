My Portfolio

You can see some of the projects I've worked on here.

ResNet-34 Implementation

I implemented a ResNet architecture from scratch, gaining a deeper understanding of the inner processes of neural networks. The structure was used to classify images of cats with different fur patterns, and reached a testing accuracy of up to 80% with 8 classes. This was a part of the UNSW Course Neural Networks (COMP9444) for which I got a High Distinction grade/

Experiences I Gained: Data Augmentation, Knowledge of Optimisers, Setting up a Pytorch Project

Taste of Research Program

After receiving the Taste of Research Scholarship from UNSW, I had the opportunity to work with a Fetch Robot. I developed a set of scenarios for participants to re-enact, and collected data regarding their movement to improve the field of Social Robotics.

Experiences I Gained: Robot Operating System (ROS) Usage, Academic Research, Academic Processes

Computer Vision

I Classified and compared the effectiveness of different methods for various computer vision tasks in a group setting. This was greatly helped by the computer vision course I took at UNSW (COMP9517) for which I had a High Distinction grade.

Experiences I Gained: Motion Tracking, Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, Image Segmentation

UR5e Programming

I coded the UR5e robot manipulators made available to UNSW students to complete various tasks, such as tracing any arbitrary set of digits at any rotation and any position in human-like fashion as pictured.

Experiences I Gained: Homogeneous Transformations, Inverse Kinematics, Jacobian Matrices, Robot Trajectories, UR5e usage
